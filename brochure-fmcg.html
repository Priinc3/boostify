Every FMCG brand has unique operational challenges. Here's how our automation solutions solve them.
1

Automated Order Processing

Orders from distributors via WhatsApp, email, or portal are automatically captured, validated against stock, and pushed to your ERP — no manual data entry.

Result: 90% faster order processing
2

Distributor & Retailer Communication

AI-powered WhatsApp bot that handles distributor queries — order status, pricing, scheme details, payment reminders — automatically, in their preferred language.

Result: 24/7 support without additional staff
3

Inventory & Stock Alerts

Real-time stock monitoring across warehouses. Automatic low-stock alerts, reorder triggers, and expiry date tracking — all synced with your ERP and messaging tools.

Result: Reduce stockouts by 60%, cut waste by 35%
4

Sales Team Reporting & CRM

Field sales reports auto-collected via WhatsApp or mobile app. Data flows directly into your CRM — visit logs, new orders, feedback — zero manual entry at end-of-day.

Result: 100% field data capture, real-time visibility
5

Scheme & Promotion Management

Automate trade scheme communication, track scheme eligibility per distributor, calculate rewards, and send personalized scheme updates via WhatsApp.

Result: 5x faster scheme rollout
6

Invoice & Payment Reconciliation
