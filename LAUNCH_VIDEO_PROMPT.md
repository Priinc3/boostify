# Boostify — Launch Video AI Prompt

Use this prompt with AI video generators like Runway Gen-3, Sora, Kling, Vidu, or Pika.

---

## MASTER PROMPT

```
Create a 60-second cinematic launch video for "Boostify" — an AI & Automation agency. The video should feel premium, modern, and Apple-like with smooth 3D animations, clean typography, and a navy blue + white color palette.

BRAND IDENTITY:
- Company: Boostify
- Tagline: "We Automate — You Elevate"
- Colors: Deep navy blue (#0a0e27), white (#ffffff), accent blue (#3b82f6), pale blue (#e8edfa)
- Font style: Modern sans-serif (Inter/SF Pro style), bold and clean
- Overall feel: Premium tech agency, minimal, sophisticated, confident

VISUAL STYLE:
- Clean white and navy blue environments
- 3D floating geometric shapes (circles, rings, diamonds, hexagons) in pale blue, slowly rotating
- Smooth camera movements — slow dolly-ins, orbits, and parallax shifts
- Glassmorphic UI elements floating in 3D space
- Soft depth-of-field and ambient lighting
- Particle effects — subtle blue dots connecting in a network pattern
- Light rays and soft glows in accent blue
- No harsh colors, no neon, no busy backgrounds
- Think Apple keynote meets Stripe landing page aesthetics

---

SCENE BREAKDOWN (60 seconds):

SCENE 1 — THE HOOK (0-8 seconds)
Visual: Dark navy background. Camera slowly pushes forward through a 3D space filled with floating geometric shapes (translucent rings, diamonds, dots) in pale blue. The shapes drift gently like they're suspended in zero gravity.
Text animation: "Your team is drowning in manual work." fades in with a clean typewriter effect, white text, centered. Holds for 2 seconds, then dissolves.
Then: "There's a better way." fades in with a slight upward motion, in accent blue (#3b82f6).
Music: Ambient electronic, soft synth pad, building anticipation. Low bass pulse.
Mood: Mysterious, intriguing, problem-aware.

SCENE 2 — THE PROBLEM (8-18 seconds)
Visual: Transition to a clean white 3D environment. Show abstract 3D representations of manual business pain points:
- A stack of floating document/spreadsheet icons multiplying endlessly (representing data entry chaos)
- Clock icons spinning faster and faster (representing wasted time)
- Disconnected puzzle pieces floating apart (representing broken workflows)
- A human silhouette surrounded by overwhelming floating task bubbles
Camera slowly orbits around these elements. Everything feels chaotic but elegant.
Text overlays (appear one at a time with smooth fade-in):
  "Hours lost copying data between spreadsheets"
  "Leads slipping through the cracks"
  "Repetitive tasks eating your team's time"
Each line appears for 2-3 seconds with subtle motion.
Music: Tension builds slightly, rhythmic pulse.

SCENE 3 — THE SOLUTION REVEAL (18-30 seconds)
Visual: Dramatic transition — all the chaotic elements from Scene 2 suddenly organize themselves. The floating documents snap into a clean pipeline/workflow. Disconnected puzzle pieces click together. The overwhelming task bubbles shrink and get absorbed into a single glowing blue orb.
The Boostify logo materializes in the center — "Boostify." in bold navy text with the blue dot, rendered in 3D with a subtle metallic/glass texture. The logo slowly rotates to reveal its dimensionality.
Below the logo, the tagline "We Automate — You Elevate" types itself out in a clean sans-serif font.
Camera does a slow cinematic orbit around the 3D logo.
Background: Clean gradient from white to pale blue, with soft light rays emanating from behind the logo.
3D floating geometric shapes (rings, diamonds) orbit gently around the logo.
Music: The beat drops — confident, uplifting electronic with clean percussion. Energy shifts from tension to empowerment.

SCENE 4 — WHAT WE DO (30-45 seconds)
Visual: Transition to a sequence of 4 mini-showcases, each lasting ~3-4 seconds. Clean white/navy environment with 3D glassmorphic UI cards floating in space.

Panel 1 — Workflow Automation:
Show a 3D animated workflow diagram — glowing nodes connected by animated blue lines, data flowing between them like electricity. Icons for email, spreadsheet, CRM visually connected.
Text: "Workflow Automation" + "Connect your tools. Eliminate manual handoffs."

Panel 2 — AI Agents & Chatbots:
Show a 3D chat interface with messages appearing automatically. An AI brain icon pulses in the center. Conversation bubbles animate in and out.
Text: "AI-Powered Agents" + "Smart bots that work 24/7 for your business."

Panel 3 — CRM & Lead Automation:
Show a 3D funnel visualization — leads flowing in from the top (social media, web, WhatsApp icons), flowing through stages, converting at the bottom into deal/revenue icons.
Text: "Lead Automation" + "Never miss a lead. Auto-follow-up. Auto-nurture."

Panel 4 — Custom Dashboards:
Show a 3D dashboard with animated charts, graphs, and numbers counting up. Data visualizations floating in space, real-time updates.
Text: "Real-Time Dashboards" + "All your data. One view. Instant insights."

Each panel transitions with a smooth 3D camera movement — either a swipe, zoom, or orbit.
Music: Continues building, confident and energetic.

SCENE 5 — RESULTS / SOCIAL PROOF (45-52 seconds)
Visual: Clean navy blue background. 3D animated stat counters appear one by one with smooth scale-up animations:
  "50+ Projects Delivered" (with a subtle glow effect)
  "5+ Happy Clients"
  "99% Success Rate"
  "24/7 Support"
Each stat appears in a glassmorphic card, floating in 3D space. Numbers count up from 0 with a satisfying animation.
Subtle particle network in the background connecting dots.
Music: Peak energy, triumphant.

SCENE 6 — CALL TO ACTION (52-60 seconds)
Visual: All elements fade away leaving a clean, minimal scene. The Boostify logo appears center-screen in its full glory — "Boostify." with the blue dot, large and bold. Soft light rays behind it.
Below: "We Automate — You Elevate" fades in.
Then: "boostifycorp.in" and "contact@boostifycorp.dev" appear below in smaller text.
The floating geometric shapes from the opening scene return — circles, rings, diamonds — orbiting gently around the logo, creating a sense of completeness (full circle).
Final frame holds for 3 seconds (thumbnail-worthy).
Music: Resolves to a clean, satisfying ending. Soft reverb tail.

---

TRANSITIONS:
- Use smooth, cinematic transitions between scenes — no hard cuts
- Morph transitions (one element transforms into the next)
- Camera-based transitions (zoom into an element, emerge in next scene)
- Particle dissolve (elements break into particles then reform)

CAMERA MOVEMENT:
- Slow, deliberate, professional
- Subtle parallax depth throughout
- Slight handheld feel for organic touch, but mostly smooth gimbal-style
- Occasional slow-motion moments for emphasis

TYPOGRAPHY RULES:
- All text: clean, modern sans-serif (Inter Black or similar)
- Headlines: bold, white or navy, large
- Subtext: lighter weight, gray or pale blue, smaller
- All text animations: fade-in with subtle upward motion (translate Y from +20px to 0)
- No text should feel rushed — each line gets breathing room

MUSIC & SOUND DESIGN:
- Style: Modern ambient electronic, similar to Apple keynote music
- Build: Starts atmospheric and mysterious, builds to confident and uplifting
- Elements: Soft synth pads, clean percussion, subtle bass, whoosh transitions
- Sound effects: Soft "click" when UI elements snap together, subtle "whoosh" on transitions, gentle "ding" on stat counter completion
- No voiceover needed — the visuals and text tell the story
- Ending: Clean resolve, not abrupt

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Keep it MINIMAL — never more than 2-3 elements on screen at once
- Every frame should be screenshot-worthy
- The overall feeling should be: "This company is premium, modern, and knows what they're doing"
- Avoid stock-footage feeling — everything should feel custom and intentional
- Color consistency is critical — stick to navy (#0a0e27), white, accent blue (#3b82f6), pale blue (#e8edfa) throughout
- No gradients except the subtle white-to-pale-blue background
- 16:9 aspect ratio, 1080p or 4K
```

## RECOMMENDED AI VIDEO TOOLS

| Tool | Best For | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Runway Gen-3 Alpha** | Full scenes, good motion | Use scene-by-scene prompts |
| **Kling AI** | 3D quality, longer clips | Great for the geometric shapes |
| **Sora (OpenAI)** | Best overall quality | Use master prompt, high coherence |
| **Pika** | Quick iterations | Good for testing individual scenes |
| **Vidu** | Stylized motion | Good for the abstract scenes |
| **HailuoAI** | Smooth transitions | Good for connecting scenes |

**Workflow tip:** Generate each scene separately (5-10 sec clips), then stitch together in CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, or Premiere Pro. Add typography overlays and music in post-production for best control.
