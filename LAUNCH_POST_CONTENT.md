## POSTING TIPS

| | Instagram | LinkedIn |
|---|---|---|
| **Best time to post** | 9-11 AM or 7-9 PM IST | 8-10 AM IST (Tue-Thu) |
| **Image size** | 1080×1080 (square) | 1200×627 (landscape) |
| **Hashtags** | 15-20 in caption | 5-8 at the end |
| **Tone** | Punchy, visual, quick | Professional, story-driven |
| **CTA** | "DM us" or "Link in bio" | "Let's connect" |
